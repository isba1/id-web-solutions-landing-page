ID Innovations Growth Engine

The Growth Engine is a backend scheduling and lead management platform built for local business websites managed by ID Innovations. It automates appointment booking, captures and routes inbound leads, and integrates with Google services (including Calendar and Gmail) to keep business owners connected with their customers — without requiring manual follow-up.

About this application

Built by ID Innovations (idinnovations.dev)
Purpose Scheduling automation and lead management for local business clients
Data accessed Google Calendar (read/write) and Gmail (send on behalf of clients) via OAuth 2.0
Audience Business owners who have engaged ID Innovations to manage their web presence
